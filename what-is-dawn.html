Dawn is a global, self-governed and open-sourced Blockchain built for gamers

Dawn is our new blockchain that we are building as a “Cosmos Zone” built on top of Tendermint and Cosmos SDK, using a bridge contract deployed on the Ethereum mainnet. Building Dawn this way means that ERC-20 tokens can be safely moved into, and later withdrawn from, the Dawn Chain.

We are happy that we have finally built our own gaming protocol. Our protocol will have multiple custom modules that allow developers to build dapp and high performance applications on top of our new chain, Dawn. Dawn is our new blockchain that we are building as a “Cosmos Zone” built on Tendermint consensus, using a bridge contract deployed on the Ethereum mainnet. Building Dawn this way means that ERC-20 tokens can be safely moved into, and later withdrawn from, the Dawn Chain.

The Tendermint architecture allows FirstBlood to scale transactions per second well above the current Ethereum limit, and at significantly less cost to end users, while keeping Ethereum as our top layer. This means that we can eliminate the scaling problems we encountered in building on Ethereum without losing its credibility as a powerful, trusted platform for maintaining token ownership and transactions.

The Cosmos network will bring a variety of benefits to users, including:

  • Lower transaction fees
  • Security of tokens
  • Frictionless user experience
  • Chance to deposit stablecoins and other assets, and take advantage of a higher TPS rate
