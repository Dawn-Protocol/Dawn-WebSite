Welcome to the Dawn Community!

We are glad you are here. We are a community of gamers, blockchain enthusiast, streamers, and entrepreneurs who love gaming and disruptive technology! We are building and developing the world’s largest community centered around decentralized esports competition. We want to put the power back in the players’ hands, and reward you for participating in our ecosystem!

As a Dawn community member, you can expect:

  • Regular and competitive esports competitions paid in our native token.
  • The opportunity to win prizes from additional giveaways, contests, promotions, AMAs, and bounties on our various social channels.
  • Register to become one of our community validators! You can get paid to operate a node on the Dawn network, and earn generous rewards.
  • Stake your Dawn tokens and earn.
  • Regular communication from the team on upcoming events, development progress, and more.
  • An updated roadmap, whitepaper, and vision for the upcoming year(s).

Again, we’d like to thank you for your support, and if you’re new, welcome! Please be sure to join all of our social channels for regular updates to follow all of the events that we have coming up. Buckle up, because it’s going to be a fun ride!
