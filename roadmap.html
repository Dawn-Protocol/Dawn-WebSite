Our Story

When we first started Firstblood Project back in 2016, our goal was to use decentralized wagering and conflict resolution to create a product that Esports gamers would love...

However, the challenge has been finding a platform that can scale to meet our needs, while accommodating a legion of passionate gamers that are eager to meet and compete. As we are quietly building applications that are used by hundreds of thousands users around the world, we also did some lots of researching, testing and building on our own solution to achieve our ultimate mission.
