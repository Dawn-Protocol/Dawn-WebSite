Roadmap

Gear your Ethereum wallet up and get ready to contribute to our upcoming Mainet Launch?

Dawn is our new blockchain that we are building as a “Cosmos Zone” built on top of Tendermint and Cosmos SDK, using a bridge contract deployed on the Ethereum mainnet. Building Dawn this way means that ERC-20 tokens can be safely moved into, and later withdrawn from, the Dawn Chain.

FirstBlood Launched in India launching Alpha of Mobile App

August 2019

We officially entered India marketing supporting popular mobile title PUBG mobile. The app has officially launched in October and have been loved my our community.

FirstBlood Launched in Vietnam and Partnered up with Divine

January 2019

With months of planning, we launched our platform in Vietnam partnering up with Divine created the first national league for PUBG.

Fully Decentralized Dapp Launched

August 2018

After learning and fine-tuning our platform, we’ve gone ahead and launched the first fully 100% decentralized platform on ethereum allowing gamers to challenge and wager our native ERC20 Token 1ST.

Adding 2nd Esports Game

April 2018

Firstblood became a reality in September of 2016, after our raise was completed in seconds with overwhelming support from the community. As a pioneer in blockchain and Gaming, we went on a journey to pursue our mission of creating an ever-rewarding experience for anyone who plays video games around the world.

Public Launch of Esports Platform

July 2017

Shortly coming out of private Alpha, we’ve officially open the access to public for our beta platform where Dota2 users can compete either in 1v1 and 5v5 queue with friends and team.
